Formula 1 The Best Racing Sport to Ever Exist

2

Extreme g-forces. Daring decisions made in the blink of an eye – and at 370km/h. Dramatically battling to be the best, Formula 1 drivers are more like fighter pilots than sportspeople. Only 10 people get the chance to compete, and they push themselves – and their incredible machines – to the very limit.

Formula 1 is a sport where 10 different car brands compete with eachother in races with their own built formula 1 car.
It's classed as a sport because the driver needs a fit body and a muscly neck for the g-force the car creates when turning at such a high speed.

Who Races in F1?

F1 The Most Famous Racing Sport

Formula 1 features a dynamic array of teams, each contributing to the sport's rich competitive landscape. Mercedes, known for their recent dominance, have secured multiple Constructors' Championships with superior engineering and driver talent. Red Bull Racing, with their aggressive strategies and strong driver lineup, consistently challenge for titles. Ferrari, the sport's oldest and most storied team, blend heritage with innovation.
